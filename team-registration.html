What happens next?
To be officially accepted into the tournament, you must send:
  • AYSO Tournament Application (PDF for 2026 OSF)
  • Official team roster
  • Region-issued check for the full tournament fee

Your team will show as Pending to the Tournament Committee until all documents and payment are received and verified.

Once approved, your team will appear on the Approved Teams list.
