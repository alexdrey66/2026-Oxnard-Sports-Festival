About the Oxnard Sports Festival

The Oxnard Sports Festival is one of AYSO’s signature tournaments in Area 10W, bringing together competitive teams, dedicated referees, and families for a weekend of quality soccer and community.

Tournament Format
Pool Play & Finals

Teams compete in pool play on Saturday with advancement to Sunday semifinals and finals, depending on division size.

AYSO Philosophy
Everyone Plays & Positive Coaching

Matches are run in alignment with AYSO’s core philosophies, emphasizing player development, safety, and fun.

Facilities
Quality Fields & On-site Support

Well-maintained fields, concessions, restrooms, medical support, and clear signage to help teams and families navigate the event.

Community
Festival Atmosphere

Beyond the games, the festival offers a family-friendly environment with food, vendors, and space for teams to gather.

Divisions & Team Slots

Each division is capped at 16 teams. Spots are confirmed once payment and paperwork are fully received.
