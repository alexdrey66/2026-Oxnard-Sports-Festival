Legend
How statuses and docs flow into the public views.
  • Pending: visible only in Admin until all documents and payment are confirmed.
  • Approved: appears on the public Approved Teams list.
  • Declined: stays in the system; can be moved back to Pending.
  • Docs checkboxes: for your tracking only; they do not affect public visibility.
